Course Framework

This course follows the I·O·W·L·H progression: (1) Know Thyself → (2) Cleanse & Purify → (3) Live in Alignment with Absolute Truth. Each week gives a principle, practice, and A–B–C emphasis.

Three SGI Paths (A–B–C)

  • A — Ilekes Path: Know Thyself through Radical Devotion (identity, alignment, authority).
  • B — Warriors Path: Cleanse & Purify through Words of Power (discipline, ethical speech, defense).
  • C — Hand of Ifá: Live in Alignment with Absolute Truth through Infinite Wisdom (destiny, divination, ancestors).
